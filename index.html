tibi chocolate range redesign

tibi is a Hungarian, traditional, mainstream tablet chocolate brand. The consumers considered tibi outdated and dull. The producer decided to renew the recipe and the packaging. The challenge was to reposition the product but keep the traditional hue at the same time. The brief called for a modern, realistic, simple pack design with appetizing taste presentation, and the old, slightly pimped brand logo. A design that you would be happy to get it as a gift and for some it still can recall nice memories.

opel - rezsicsökkentés (public utility fee cut)

The Hungarian Government reduced the centrally regulated household price of electricity, gas and district heating by a total of 20 percent in 2013. This advertisement outlines the benefits of using electric cars and the reduction of utilities. This is a humorous representation of the effect of this hungarian legislation about public fee cut.
